Project 3
TRUEresult Glucose Meter Usability Test & Analysis
This small project is documentation of some of the usability testing that I have been involved in. Three participants were asked to use a glucose meter to perform three tasks, all of which were determined to be essential to the process of monitoring blood glucose levels. The testing yielded a document containing general findings as well as very broad suggestions for improving the usability of the glucose meter.
