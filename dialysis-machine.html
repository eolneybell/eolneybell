Project 2
Portable Dialysis Machine Concept
I worked with two other students to develop a concept for a peritoneal dialysis machine designed to free patients from the normally captive experience of dialysis treatment. The concept included a backpack to contain the dialysis machine plus a mobile app and wrist controller for monitoring vitals, keeping track of treatments, and communicating with health care professionals. Among the human factors processes involved in this project, building FMEAs and conducting cognitive walkthroughs with dialysis patients were particularly educational.
